About Me

This is a little about me and my quest to cultivate my dream life.

I am a sweet, young girl who is on the hunt for love. I'm 5'5", hazel eyes, brownish blonde hair, and an athletic build. I would ideally like a man who has dreamy eyes, a good head of hair, and can handle my spunky personality!

I bring lots of great qualities to the table. I am quite the neat freak, and will keep any place spick and span. I am a self starter who is destined to be the bread winner - don't worry, I'll be your sugarmama. I am smart, thoughtful, and witty. I also know how to bake a great chocolate chip cookie. Still improving my cooking skills though...

My ultimate dream is to have a white, mediterranean style house akin to Cameron Diaz's house in The Holiday with two golden retrievers named beau and belle laying on the front lawn and a chalk grey Porsche Macan parked in the garage. If you believe you have something to offer to this dream, please continue to explore my website!
